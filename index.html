Which AI tools does this support?

AI Export Studio natively supports exports for ChatGPT (including Canvas and o1 models) and Google Gemini (Advanced).

Will my code blocks and tables look correct?

Yes! Unlike messy copy-pasting, we preserve the exact formatting of your chats. Code blocks, tables, bold text, and lists will render perfectly in your PDF or Word document.

Are my conversations private?

100% private. The extension operates entirely within your browser natively. None of your chat data is ever sent to an external server. (Note: Opt-in feedback & bug reports only collect safe diagnostic info, never your chat content).

Absolutely. Your Lifetime Deal includes all future updates, bug fixes, and new features we add to the Pro version. You pay once and own it forever.

Yes, your Pro license allows you to activate the extension on up to 3 personal devices (like your work laptop, home PC, and a backup device).
