# AI Export Studio 🚀

The elegant way to save ChatGPT & Gemini conversations to PDF, Word, and Markdown. No copy-paste. No mess.

## ✨ Features
- **Flawless Fidelity:** LaTeX, tables, and code blocks render exactly as they do in your browser.
- **Export Everywhere:** Save formats exactly how you need them—as pristine **PDF**, **Word (.docx)**, **Markdown**, **HTML**, or even **JSON**.
- **Total Privacy:** Your chats never leave your machine. Everything is processed 100% locally on your device.
- **Selective Focus:** Cut out the noise. Select only the specific prompts and responses you need.
- **Deep Capture:** Automatically scrolls and captures massive conversations without missing a single detail.
